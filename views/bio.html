Name

Agaroth band’s name was taken from J.R.R. Tolkien’s mythology, the
name is a mixture between “Agar” that means blood and “goroth” that
means terror, the pronunciation is as it is written

Bio

Agaroth band was born on February 21st 2013, first formed by Mauricio
as a guitar player and Jerónimo in the drums, the first attempt as a death
metal band was not enough and we decided to include the dark touch of
black metal, after few months the first singer left the band and our guitar
player became the singer also. Our project is shaped by the harshness of
black metal and the crunch of death metal with decadent touches, but
slightly melodic, our lyrics are about misanthropy, death, anti religion,
anti-life, the decay and destruction of the human being.
